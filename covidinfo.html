COVID-19 Information

COVID-19, or Coronavirus, is a deadly virus that has affected the entire world. People are losing their jobs and homes due to the virus. Learn how you can stop the spread below + more information.

How to Slow the Spread

  • Wash your hands frequently, for at least 20 seconds.
  • Always wear a mask in public.
  • Social distance: stay six feet apart.
  • Try to stay at home.
  • Sanitize after going to a public area.
  • Wipe down surfaces, clean your home.
  • Shop, host meetings, and work online
  • Test for COVID-19
